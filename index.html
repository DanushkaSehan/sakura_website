私たちについて - About Us

Over a Decade of Japanese Education

The center was founded in 2014 by Yamada Ruwani with the purpose of helping students learn Japanese and prepare for N5, N4, N3, N2, and N1 examinations - the mandatory certifications to work or study in Japan.

There are 10 courses available in SJLC, suitable for every skill level. We also provide consultations on student visas and career guidance for students planning to move to Japan.

What Makes Us Different

  • We use "Minna no Nihongo" textbooks to build a solid Japanese foundation.
  • Level up from beginner to advanced courses step by step.
  • Preparation for O/L, A/L, and JLPT examinations.
  • Career consulting for technical school and university applications in Japan.
  • Cultural events and activities to experience real Japanese life.
