Our Courses

N5 - Basic Level

100 KANJI + 800 VOCABULARY WORDS

N5 is the most basic level of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test and just requires you to understand some basic Japanese.
The N5 level basically ensures you can understand basic sentences and typical expressions written in hiragana, katakana, and basic kanji as well as from spoken conversations.

N4 - Elementary Level

300 KANJI + 1500 VOCABULARY WORDS

N4, while slightly more advanced, has the same description, to understand some basic Japanese.
The N4 part of the JLPT however focuses on more common daily interactions.

N3 - Intermediate Level

650 KANJI + 3700 VOCABULARY WORDS

N3 is the intermediate level of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test and is described as the ability to understand Japanese used in everyday situations to a certain degree.
This is slightly more advanced than the previous levels in that spoken conversations are to be understood at near-natural speed rather than slow and understand more day-to-day writings such as in newspaper headlines.

N2 - Pre-Advanced Level

1,000 KANJI + 6,000 VOCABULARY WORDS

N2 is where things start to get, again, more complicated and is described as the ability to understand Japanese in everyday situations, and in a variety of circumstances to a certain degree.
As one of the highest levels of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test, you are required to not only understand a handful of general topics, but comprehend the intent and narrative behind them.

N1 - Advanced Level

2,000 KANJI + 10,000 VOCABULARY WORDS

N1 is the advanced level and therefore the most complicated, and is described as the ability to understand Japanese used in a variety of circumstances.
At the highest level of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test, you are required to be able to read and listen with logical complexity and comprehend both the structures and content comprehensively.

About Us

The center was founded in 2014 by Yamada Ruwani with the purpose of helping students to learn the Japanese language and prepare for examinations like N5, N4, N3, N2, and N1. These are the mandatory examinations to prove your fluency in Japanese in order for you to move to Japan for work or study.


Altogether, there are 10 courses available in SJLC which are suitable for every skill levels starting from the beginner to more advanced users of the Japanese Language. During these years, we have helped so many students to improve their language skills and pass the tests successfully.

We also provide consultations in student visa-related matters for those who are in need of help with finding and applying for a Japanese School.


Features of our School

  • Level up to Intermediate & Advanced course
  • Preparation for the O/L, A/L and Japanese Language Proficiency Tests
